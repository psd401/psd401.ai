## Prompt Used

Prompt 1:

```md
I am a 7th grade social studies teacher. Our students have traditionally scored lower in interpreting informational text. Help me write a student growth goal around nonfiction and informational reading and interpretation based on the following: In Washington, the teacher must set specific, measurable goals for individual students or subgroups of students based on their learning needs, identifying areas where they need to make the most progress academically; this is considered a key part of differentiated instruction and personalized learning.
```

Prompt 2:

```md
How would I collect data to prove improvement on the goal above
```

Prompt 3:

```md
I need a google sheet to use to analyze data regarding students' progress
```
